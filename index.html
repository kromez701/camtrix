Frequently Asked Questions

How Easy Is It To Install And Set Up CamTrix?

Its very easy to install and set up CamTrix. In fact, you can do it in under 3 minutes! After unpacking, scan the QR code on the side of the box to download the camera app and follow the instructions to connect it to your phone and WiFi. After its connected, use either a 360 magnetic camera mount or the sticky tape (which comes in the package) to place it where you need it the most.

Can It Be Placed Upside Down?

Yes, thanks to its compact design and magnetic surface, you can place the CamTrix camera upside down. The direction of its placement will not affect its performance.

Can I Use CamTrix Outdoors?

Yes, you can place CamTrix outdoors to monitor your driveway, doorstep, or yard.

How Often Should I Charge It?

The app, connected to the camera, will send a notification informing you that the camera should be recharged. The frequency of charging will depend on your camera settings.

Do I Have To Manually Switch It To Night Mode?

No, CamTrix automatically switches between day and night modes by using a smart light sensor.
